Sales & Inventory Management System - User Tutorial

1. Getting Started

1
Access the App: Open the PartFlow Pro app on your Android device.
2
Login: Use your assigned Username and Password.
Default Admin: admin / admin123
3
Dashboard: The home screen shows you quick actions: Start Order, Sync Data, and View Reports.

2. Creating a New Order

1
Select a Shop: Go to the Shops tab. Search for the customer and tap their card to start a new order.
2
Add Items: Use the search bar or QR Scanner to find spare parts. Tap an item to set the quantity.
3
Review Order: Your current total and discount (auto-applied from shop settings) are shown at the bottom.
4
Confirm: Once finished, tap Confirm Order.

3. Invoices & Sharing

1
Preview: After confirmation, a professional invoice is generated.
2
Share to Customer: Tap the Share button. This opens the Android menuâ€”select WhatsApp or Email to send the PDF directly to the shop owner.
3
History: You can always find past orders in the History tab to re-print or view details.

4. Data Sync (Cloud Connection)

Important: Always sync at the end of the day when you have an internet connection to ensure HQ receives your orders.
1
Sync Dashboard: Tap the Sync icon in the top right bar.
2
Sync & Push: Tap Sync & Push to upload all your local orders to the master Excel sheet and download the latest stock quantities.
3
Download Only: If you just want to refresh the inventory list from HQ without uploading anything, use the Download Latest Master Record link.

5. Business Reports

1
Reports View: Tap the Reports icon in the top bar.
2
Drill Down: Tap any card (e.g., Period Revenue) to see the full list of transactions. Tap a Shop name to see their specific purchase history.
3
Download PDF: Use the Download PDF Report button to generate a professional document of your performance for the selected date range.

6. App Settings

1
Company Details: Tap the Settings icon. Here you can change the Company Name, Address, and Invoice Prefix that appear on the bills.
2
Logout: Safely sign out of the app. Your local data (orders not yet synced) will remain on the device.
